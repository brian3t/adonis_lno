Music at the Shoppes

The Shoppes at Carlsbad
2525 El Camino Real, Carlsbad, 92008
Get Directions
Cost: Free

As residents take advantage of outdoor dining options and warmer and longer days, now is the perfect time for music al fresco. To enhance and elevate the patio dining experience, Music at the Shoppes returns on Saturday evenings throughout March. Guests can enjoy live performances of jazz, folk and pop artists on the outdoor patio/open space in front of Regal Cinemas. Various dining tenants will be offering special deals and promotions throughout the music series. March 6: Tim Rose (lounge, pop, top 40). March 13: Jemille Vialet (piano, smooth jazz)/ March 20: Blaise Guld (top 40, pop, folk). March 27: Jesse Ray (Americana, soul, classic rock)

Share

When

Ongoing until Saturday, March 27, 2021

Hours

Saturdays, 5pm-8pm
